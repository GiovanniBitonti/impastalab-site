Corporate Experiences at Impasta Lab

At Impasta Lab, we bring teams together through the joy of Italian food — hands-on, sensory, and full of flavour. Our corporate experiences are designed to inspire connection, creativity, and teamwork in a relaxed, authentic setting. Whether you’re kneading dough, guessing secret ingredients, or sharing a handmade meal, each activity blends tradition, fun, and a true taste of Italy.

1. “Mani in Pasta” – From Scratch Experience

  • Make pasta dough from scratch, roll and shape it by hand
  • Enjoy an aperitivo (focaccia, mini panzerotti, olives)
  • Take home their own handmade pasta

Includes all ingredients, tools, setup, clean-down, and take-home boxes.

🕒 Duration: ~2 hours

2. “Cuoci e Gusta” – Cook & Enjoy Experience

  • Shape and cook pre-made dough
  • Prepare and enjoy pasta with a fresh tomato & Parmesan sauce

Includes sauce, ingredients, tools, setup, and guidance.

🕒 Duration: ~1.5 hours

3. “Sfida del Gusto” – The Italian Taste Challenge

  • Aperitivo platter served while teams play “Guess the Ingredients”
  • Blind-taste Italian sauces, herbs, cheeses, and flavours
  • Lighthearted, educational, and team-building

Includes setup, hosting, clean-down, and flavour insights.

🕒 Duration: 1–1.5 hours
