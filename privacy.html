Privacy Policy

At ImpastaLab, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website, attend our workshops, or order our services.

1. What We Collect
  • Name and contact details (email, phone)
  • Order and enquiry details
  • Any dietary requirements or notes you voluntarily provide
2. How We Use Your Information
  • To process bookings, orders, or workshop registrations
  • To respond to your enquiries
  • To improve our customer experience and services
  • For occasional marketing communications (only with your consent)
3. Sharing & Security

We never sell your information. Data is stored securely and only shared with trusted partners (like payment or booking platforms) when necessary to deliver our services.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Just email us at impastalab@icloud.com.

5. Cookies

Our site uses cookies to enhance user experience and track website performance. You can disable cookies via your browser settings.

6. Updates

This policy may be updated occasionally. We’ll post any changes here and update the “Last Updated” date below.
